About Us

Discover our journey from humble beginnings to becoming one of India's leading textile manufacturers

Our Story

Jiwarajka Textile Industries is one of India's leading polyester yarn manufacturers, established in 1985. With state-of-the-art units in Silvassa and Daman, and a production capacity exceeding 1,05,000 tons annually, we serve global textile demands with quality and precision.

Our commitment to innovation, sustainability, and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted name in the textile industry for over four decades.
